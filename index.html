The Problem Nobody Sees

You may serve the territory.
Can customers discover you there?

Many businesses serve dozens of towns, cities, and neighborhoods while customer opportunities come from only a small percentage of them. That is not always a demand problem. It may be a Territory Presence problem.

01 · Invisible Territories

Serving an area is not the same as being discoverable there.

A contractor may work across 25 towns while most phone calls, form submissions, estimates, and sales conversations come from only 3 or 4. The business can serve the territory, but customers may not have a clear pathway to discover it.
